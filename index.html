EL POLLO LOCO

The Legend of Pepe

In the heart of a quaint Mexican village, lives Pepe, a humble yet courageous villager known for his quick reflexes and unshakable bravery. One fateful day, the peaceful life of the village is thrown into chaos when a swarm of unruly wild chickens descends upon the town, causing havoc wherever they go.


With a heart full of determination, Pepe steps forward to confront the feathery menace. Armed with his sharp wits and agile movements, he takes on the challenge to protect his home and restore harmony to the village. Through his daring actions, Pepe becomes a local legend, facing the feathered threat with unmatched courage to ensure the safety of his beloved community.


As the villagers cheer him on, Pepe fights not just to defend his village, but to remind everyone that true heroism can rise from the most unexpected of places.
